About Calawag Mountain Resort

Nestled in the heart of Tibiao, Antique, Calawag Mountain Resort offers an unforgettable escape into nature's embrace. Surrounded by lush mountains and the serene river, our resort is the perfect destination for adventures and relaxation. Whether you're indulging in a soothing kawa hot bath, exploring the thrills of river tubing, or trekking to nearby waterfalls, every moment here connects you to the beauty and culture of Antique. At Calawag Mountain Resort, we take pride in delivering not only an exceptional getaway but also authentic Filipino experience, rooted in local traditions and eco-friendly practices. Come, immerse yourself in tranquility and adventure at our mountain haven.
